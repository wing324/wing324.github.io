P1 - How Did Hawaii Form[SA]

Posted on 2020-04-25   |   In English   |  

Source

听写文本

Hawaii sits in the middle of the Pacific Ocean
And nowhere near the edge of a tectonic plate where volcanos are usually found
So how did the island chain form with all of its volcanos
The island chain is located over a hot spot fueled by mantle plume of heated rock
Solid rock at the bottom of the earth’s mantle is heated by planet’s core
That hot rock slowly rises and pushes up the crust forming a bulge
Under that bulge, some of the rock melts into magma
Magma makes its way to the surface through cracks in the crust
Once it’s above ground, it known as lava
Hawaii volcanos produce lava flows made of melted basalt
Which is more liquid than lava produced by more explosive volcanos like Mount St. Helens
Basaltic lava flows from volcanos with gently sloping sides
a lake of liquid lava can sometimes be seen at the top of active volcanos like Kilauea
Sometimes the magma can flow underground to the sides of main vents and erupt out of cracks in the ground called fissure
When lava meets ocean, the island grows, but you may not want to get too close
That steam is hydrochloric acid laced with tiny shards of volcanic glass
The Pacific plate is moving to the northwest, dragging the crust over the hot spot and creating islands as it goes
The hot spot itself might be moving too
The volcanos on Niihau and Kauai
The northwestern most island in the chain are about 5 million years old
The big island of Hawaii is the youngest and started forming about 400,000 years ago
It`s still changing and is home to 4 active volcanos
Eventually, the big island will no longer be over the hot spot and a new island will grow next to it
But maps won’t need updating for a while
Loihi won’t break the ocean surface for another 200,000 years, give or take.
For Scientific American
I am Kelsey Kennedy

听写翻译

夏威夷坐落在太平洋的中间
靠近地壳板块的边缘，通常在那里可以发现火山
所以拥有这些火山的岛群是如何形成的呢
这个岛群位于由炽热岩石的地幔热柱推动形成的热点(hot spot)的上方
在地幔底部的固体岩石被地核加热
炽热的岩石慢慢上升并推动地壳凸起
在凸起的下面，有些岩石融化成岩浆
岩浆通过地壳的缝隙到达地面
一旦它到达了地面，就成为了火山岩
夏威夷火山的火山岩是由玄武岩融化而成的
它比其他爆发的火山更有流动性，如Mount St. Helens
玄武岩的火山岩沿着平缓的坡度从火山中流出
有时流动的火山岩可以从活跃火山的顶部看到，如Kilauea
有时岩浆从地下流到主要的通风口并从叫做裂痕的裂缝中喷出到地面
当火山岩遇到海洋时，岛会成长，但你也许不想太靠近它
那个水蒸气是掺杂着火山玻璃小碎片的盐酸
太平洋板块向西北方向移动，拖着地壳通过热点地区，并且创造了岛屿
热点区域本身也可能是运动的
Niihau火山和kauai火山，这个岛群中最西北的火山大概有5百万年了
夏威夷上最大也是最年轻的岛大约在400,000年前开始形成
它拥有4个火山并且一直在改变
最终这些大岛屿将不再是热点区域，一个新的岛屿将会在它们旁边形成
但是地图不需要立刻改变
Loihi在大约200,000年之内是不会破土而出的
来自Scientific American
